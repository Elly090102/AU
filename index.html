Colonial Botany - Seminar

This seminar explores the research behind the *Colonial Botany* exhibition, highlighting vast botanical data from Denmark’s colonial era and examining how science and colonialism shaped plant collection across centuries.

The future man

The year is 2035 — the future is here! Technology is everywhere, offering solutions for everything from depression to lost pets. Flying cars, climate fixes, and even eternal life may no longer be fiction — in 2035, it's all just a choice away.

Beetles

Art & Science: Beetles! is an exhibition celebrating the shared passion for immersion between artists and scientists. Visual artist Birgit Bjerre explores the museum’s collections and finds striking parallels between her creative process and the meticulous work of researchers.

The Global Backyard

The museum's large exhibition is about the incredible myriad of life that exists on Earth. Mostly about the life we know, but also about everything we don't know yet and about species that we will never get to know, because they will be extinct before we have time to discover them!
